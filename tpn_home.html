PLOT

Emma, Ray and Norman live in Grace Field, an orphanage, run by a kind woman who they call "Mama" Isabella, with other orphans they treat as siblings, making up a big happy family. Life up until they were 12 was all they could ever want; shelter, food, and warmth. Trying to find Connie, they horrifyingly stumble into the truth to the place they once called home. Questioning their whole existence, they use their quick wittedness, skills and intelligence to escape their predetermined fates.

INFO

The Promised Neverland (2016) is a shonen, dark fantasy, sci-fi, and thriller manga written by Kaiu Shirai and beautifully illustrated by Posuka Demizu. Spawining a four volume light Novel two years (2018) after its first publication, and an anime series (2018) subsequently after that of the same names. Even after its manga finale, a light novel details Emma, Norman and Ray reminsing their memories after it.

Imagine, you as a coal miner. A shiny spec of light hits your swollen eyes, you pick it up with your scar ridden and coal blackened hands, seeing a literal diamond in the rough, a coal to be polished, to be made into a diamond. You take it to professionals, hopeful for a chance to be amazed by a real diamond. All goes well, it’s half made, a few cracks and scratches need to be sheared but it’s a real diamond.

However, after waiting, and waiting, and — You get a call, from the professionals. They’ve decided to make some adjustments, you say “okay”. A year passes by. You go to the shop. They’ve now crushed, broken and displayed your diamond. You call other people and they agree that its now been ruined. But, you can’t do anything, that’s nothing now, its only partly recognizable as your diamond.

That is The Promised Neverland to me.
